About UPS Protection Sdn Bhd

Leading electrical testing, commissioning, and maintenance specialists in Malaysia since 2000

History & Philosophy

UPS Protection Sdn. Bhd. was founded in 2001 by Mr. Lau Sze Chuan who is the Managing Director. After years of effort and contribution towards the business, currently we are one of the leading electrical service providers in Sarawak.

Upon the growth of our company, we have established two branches in Bintulu and Miri as to enhance our job efficiency and effectiveness.

Our core activity is to provide engineering services in Maintenance, Testing, Commissioning, of Power Station and Substation and Oil and Gas Electrical Installations up to 275KV. For the past few years, we have completed more than 1,000 of projects throughout Malaysia and Brunei Darussalam.
